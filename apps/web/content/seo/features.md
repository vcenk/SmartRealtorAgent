# Features Built for Real Estate Professionals

Smart Realtor Agent is not a generic chatbot. Every feature is designed for real estate agents, brokers, and teams who need accurate, trustworthy AI assistance.

---

## Citation-Backed Responses

Every factual answer references your actual content.

- Responses are grounded in your knowledge base
- Citations show where information comes from
- Reduces AI hallucinations and misinformation
- Builds trust with website visitors

Your chatbot speaks from your data, not imagination.

---

## Intelligent Lead Qualification

Capture structured lead data, not just emails.

- Detect buyer vs. seller intent automatically
- Collect budget, timeline, and location preferences
- Qualify leads before they reach your inbox
- Store structured data in your dashboard

Turn casual visitors into pipeline-ready opportunities.

---

## Knowledge Base Management

Upload and manage your content easily.

- **Web Crawler**: Automatically index your website pages
- **File Upload**: Support for PDF, TXT, MD, CSV files
- **URL Import**: Add individual pages or listings
- **Re-indexing**: Update content as listings change

Your AI stays current with your business.

---

## Multi-Tenant Architecture

Built for teams and brokerages.

- Each agent or office gets isolated data
- Separate knowledge bases per tenant
- Independent lead databases
- Secure access controls

Scale from solo agent to enterprise brokerage.

---

## Customizable Widget Themes

Match your brand identity.

- **Modern Dark**: Purple-blue gradient, glass effects
- **Clean Minimal**: White background, professional look
- **Professional**: Navy with sky-blue accents
- **Glassmorphism**: Frosted glass, translucent bubbles

One script tag, multiple style options.

---

## Easy Website Embedding

Deploy in minutes, not days.

```html
<script
  src="https://yoursite.com/api/widget-script"
  data-bot-id="your-tenant-id"
  data-theme="dark"
></script>
```

No complex integrations. No developer required.
